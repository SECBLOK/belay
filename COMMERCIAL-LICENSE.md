## When you need a commercial license

A commercial license from Secblok is required if your use falls outside what the
AGPL permits:

- **Hosted or managed service without AGPL source release.** If you offer
  Belay as a hosted or managed service and do not make the complete
  corresponding source code available to users as required by AGPL §13
  (the "network use is distribution" clause), you need a commercial license.

- **Proprietary components.** The fleet management, central-management,
  compliance reporting, and curated-feed components of Belay are
  proprietary and are not covered by the AGPL repository.  Access to and use
  of these components requires a commercial license regardless of how you
  deploy them.
